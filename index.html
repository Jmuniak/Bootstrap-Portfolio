About Me

My name is Jeremy Muniak, I'm a professional recruiter from Portland, ME with an interested in full stack dev, product dev, and gaming. I grew up on a small island farm in Casco Bay and love sailing and boating but I actually hate seafood despite being an islander.

.col-xs-6 .col-md-4 I have experience in both technical and executive talent acquisiton, software sales, insurance sales, and I'm new to Full Stack Dev but enjoy it immensely(so far). I've found Dev work to be akin to woodworking in the sense that you're able to see your work come to life and get instant tangible results through problem solving.

.col-xs-6 .col-md-4 My hobbies include; disc golfing, making cold brew, board sports, video games, wood working, grilling, comedy shows, science fiction. I have built a gaming PC and have a flight simulator set up at home so I can fly spaceships in Star Citizen when I have free time.
